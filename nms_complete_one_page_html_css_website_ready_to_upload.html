About Us

New Muslim Support (NMS) is a non‑profit organization dedicated to spreading the truth about Islam to non‑Muslims and helping new Muslims feel supported and guided on their journey of faith.

Vision

A world where every person who embraces Islam feels loved, supported, and empowered to live their faith confidently.

Mission

Provide authentic Islamic knowledge to non‑Muslims and comprehensive support to new Muslims, including mentorship, resources, and community programs.

Values

  • Authenticity: Qur’an & Sunnah.
  • Compassion: Care like family.
  • Community: Safe spaces to grow.
  • Dawah: Invite with wisdom.
